# Scenario Map

Use this file to classify vague real-world plans.

## 1. Travel and mobility
Triggers:
- Flights, trains, taxis, driving, rideshare, long commutes, border crossings

High-signal factors:
- Night travel, solo travel, unfamiliar place, poor transit fallback, expensive luggage, fatigue, extreme weather, unstable driver or vehicle cues

## 2. Workplace or site visit
Triggers:
- Plant, lab, construction site, warehouse, field inspection, client site

High-signal factors:
- Chemicals, fumes, dust, noise, heat, radiation, biological agents, ladders, long walking, PPE uncertainty, emergency access limits

## 3. Health-sensitive activity
Triggers:
- Pregnancy, possible pregnancy, post-op recovery, chronic illness, acute symptoms, medication side effects

High-signal factors:
- Exertion, dehydration, heat, infection exposure, chemicals, altitude, sleep deprivation, limited bathroom access, no medical support

## 4. Stranger interaction or relationship meeting
Triggers:
- Dates, meeting online contacts, buyers, sellers, recruiters, private interviews

High-signal factors:
- Private location, alcohol, isolation, pressure, transport dependency, identity not verified, gifts or money requests

## 5. Housing or property viewing
Triggers:
- Rentals, viewings, sublets, roommate meetups, off-market offers

High-signal factors:
- Deposit before viewing, pressure to decide fast, isolated viewing, unclear ownership, document requests, no companion

## 6. Transaction, payment, or asset transfer
Triggers:
- Deposits, wire transfers, crypto, escrow, gift cards, cash carry, document handoff

High-signal factors:
- Urgency, secrecy, new payee, non-reversible payment, identity mismatch, weak verification, too-good-to-be-true value

## 7. Caregiving or dependent protection
Triggers:
- Children, elders, disability support, patient transport, temporary caregivers

High-signal factors:
- Unreliable handoff, no backup, medication timing, wandering risk, communication limits, weak emergency plan

## 8. Outdoor or environmental exposure
Triggers:
- Hiking, camping, boating, beaches, heat waves, storms, flood zones

High-signal factors:
- Poor signal, no shade, no water, solo outing, unstable forecast, wildlife, terrain difficulty, late return window

## 9. Nightlife or isolated-time movement
Triggers:
- Late trains, bars, clubs, early-morning departures, walking alone after dark

High-signal factors:
- Intoxication, dead phone, poor lighting, unfamiliar route, no live location sharing, transport dependence

## 10. Online-to-offline conversion
Triggers:
- Moving an online conversation into real-world meeting or payment

High-signal factors:
- Identity gaps, story inconsistency, urgency, private venue, requests for upfront money or documents

## 11. Business trip or multi-day travel
Triggers:
- Work trips, conferences, client visits, multi-day travel to another city, staying at hotels

High-signal factors:
- Unfamiliar city, long duration, fatigue from travel, carrying valuables or equipment, colleague dependency, time zone changes, jet lag, language barriers, visa or border issues, hotel security concerns

## 12. Digital fraud, scam, or phishing risk
Triggers:
- Receiving suspicious links, unexpected payment requests, impersonation messages, "your account is locked" alerts, romance scams, investment pitches, tech support scams, crypto schemes

High-signal factors:
- Urgency or threats, requests for money or credentials, identity inconsistencies, pressure to act now, unusual payment methods, grammar errors combined with urgency, unsolicited contact, too-good-to-be-true returns
