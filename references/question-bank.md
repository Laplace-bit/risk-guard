# Question Bank

Ask only what could change the recommendation.
Start with the smallest useful set.

## Universal High-Yield Questions

1. What exactly are you planning to do, where, and for how long?
2. Will you be alone, and can you leave independently at any point?
3. Do you have any health, pregnancy, recovery, fatigue, or medication factors that make this less routine for you?
4. What is the hardest thing to reverse if this goes badly: injury, health harm, money loss, document exposure, or being stranded?

## Travel and mobility
- What time of day is the travel, and what is your fallback if the first plan fails?
- Are you carrying valuables, sensitive documents, or anything hard to replace?
- Is the area familiar and well-served, or isolated with weak transport options?

## Workplace or site visit
- What hazards could be present: chemicals, fumes, dust, heat, noise, radiation, infection, heights?
- What PPE or safety controls are confirmed, not assumed?
- How much walking, standing, lifting, or ladder use is expected?
- If you needed to stop immediately, could you do that without pressure?

## Pregnancy or health-sensitive activity
- Could you be pregnant, recently pregnant, post-op, or managing a condition that changes your tolerance?
- Are there chemicals, infection exposure, heavy exertion, heat, dehydration, or long standing involved?
- Do you have recent symptoms or a clinician's restriction that makes this non-routine?

## Stranger meeting or date
- How well is the person verified beyond chat?
- Is the first meeting in a public place with your own transport both ways?
- Will anyone know where you are and when to check in?
- Is there pressure to drink, go somewhere private, or change plans suddenly?

## Housing or property viewing
- Have you verified the listing and the person's authority over the property?
- Are they asking for money, documents, or personal data before a normal viewing flow?
- Will the meeting happen in daylight and in a public or populated context?

## Payments and asset transfer
- Is the payment reversible?
- Why does it need to happen now rather than after independent verification?
- Have the recipient identity, ownership, and terms been verified through a second channel?

## Caregiving
- Who is vulnerable here, and what do they need on a bad day rather than a good day?
- What is the failure mode if transport, the caregiver, or timing breaks?
- Is there a backup person and emergency contact path?

## Outdoor exposure
- What are the temperature, weather, route difficulty, and return time?
- What is your water, shade, signal, and rescue plan?
- Are you solo or with someone who can realistically help?
